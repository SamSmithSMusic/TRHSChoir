UNA VOCE CHOIR

Una Voce is Thunder Ridge High Schoolâ€™s non-auditioned mixed choir. This choir is only available 3rd trimester 1st period, and is the combination of Soprano/Alto Choir and Tenor/Bass Choir from Trimester 1 and 2. It is highly recommended that you participate in those individual choirs for a trimester or two before joining the Una Voce Choir. That is where you will learn important basic music reading skills that will help you when you join Una Voce. This choir will even have the opportunity to participate in the Intermediate Choral Festival in May, and is good preparation for auditioning for our more advanced choirs the next year.
